# MyLife App — 3-Minute Demo Video Script (Conversational & Oral)

This script is optimized for a short, fast-paced, and engaging 3-minute video presentation. The tone is enthusiastic, natural, and conversational, focusing on showing off the app's real functionality.

---

## 📅 Presentation Overview

| Segment | Timing | Screen Focus | Key Actions to Demonstrate |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Intro & Dashboard** | `00:00 - 00:30` | Welcome Screen $\rightarrow$ Home Dashboard | Quick welcome, login flow, dynamic header greeting, and quick stat chips. |
| **2. Family & Alerts** | `00:30 - 00:55` | Family Directory (`/family`) | Show relationship filters, add a contact, and toggle birthday alerts. |
| **3. Smart Shopping & Tasks** | `00:55 - 01:30` | Shopping (`/shopping`) $\rightarrow$ To-Do (`/todo`) | Demonstrate automatic categorization, bulk adding, and checking off subtasks. |
| **4. Bills & Finance** | `01:30 - 02:05` | Utility (`/utility`) $\rightarrow$ Finance (`/finance`) | Point out visual bill statuses, net balance, and the smart budget warning banner. |
| **5. Voice Event Planner** | `02:05 - 02:40` | Future Events (`/future-event`) | Type a natural sentence to show NLP auto-parsing an event with a countdown. |
| **6. Sync & Wrap-up** | `02:40 - 03:00` | Settings Screen $\rightarrow$ Home | Explain spouse syncing, summarize value, and sign off. |

---

## 🎙️ Detailed Scene Script

### Segment 1: Introduction & Dashboard
**Timing:** `00:00 - 00:30` (30 seconds)  
**Visuals:** Start on the clean login screen $\rightarrow$ Enter credentials $\rightarrow$ Load the **Home Dashboard**.  
**On-Screen Action:**
1. Tap **Sign In** to navigate to the Home screen.
2. Hover or point cursor over the dynamic greeting (*"Good morning, John 👋"*) and the three summary stat chips (Shopping, Tasks, Upcoming).

> **Narrator (Spoken):**
> "Hey everyone! Keeping track of a busy household usually means juggling five different apps, endless text threads, and missed reminders. 
> 
> That's why we built **MyLife**—your family’s central command center. Let’s sign in and take a look.
> 
> Right away, the dashboard greets you with a dynamic welcome banner and today's date. At a glance, these top stat chips show you exactly what's on your plate today: items left to shop, pending tasks, and upcoming events. No clutter, just the essentials."

---

### Segment 2: Family Directory & Birthday Alerts
**Timing:** `00:30 - 00:55` (25 seconds)  
**Visuals:** Tap on the **Family** card to open `/family`.  
**On-Screen Action:**
1. Quick tap between the **All**, **Immediate**, and **Extended** filters.
2. In the quick-add bar, add a family member: *Jane (Wife)*.
3. Toggle the **Bell Icon** on her card to enable birthday notifications.

> **Narrator (Spoken):**
> "Let’s start with the **Family Directory**. It’s more than just a list of contacts; it keeps your family connected. 
> 
> You can filter members in a tap, and adding someone is super fast. I'll add my wife, Jane, here. 
> 
> See this bell icon? Toggling it tells MyLife to schedule a automatic birthday notification, so you’ll never forget to pick up flowers again!"

---

### Segment 3: Smart Shopping & Subtask Progress
**Timing:** `00:55 - 01:30` (35 seconds)  
**Visuals:** Go back to Home $\rightarrow$ Open **Shopping** (`/shopping`) $\rightarrow$ Switch to **To-Do** (`/todo`).  
**On-Screen Action:**
1. In Shopping, type *"Milk"* $\rightarrow$ show it auto-assign to *"Groceries"*.
2. Open the bulk-add input and enter *"Bread, Bandages"* $\rightarrow$ show them auto-categorized into Groceries and Medicine.
3. Switch to **To-Do**, click a task to expand it, check off a subtask, and point to the progress bar filling up.

> **Narrator (Spoken):**
> "Now, onto **Shopping**. We’ve integrated a smart categorization engine here. Watch this: when I type 'Milk', the app automatically slots it under 'Groceries'. 
> 
> If you have a long list, you can just dump it in the bulk-add box—like 'Bread, Bandages'—and MyLife instantly sorts them. 
> 
> Over in **To-Do**, tasks are just as smart. You can break down complex chores into subtasks. As you check them off, this visual progress bar fills up, letting you see exactly how close you are to done."

---

### Segment 4: Bills Tracking & Finance Alerts
**Timing:** `01:30 - 02:05` (35 seconds)  
**Visuals:** Open **Utility** (`/utility`) $\rightarrow$ Switch to **Finance** (`/finance`).  
**On-Screen Action:**
1. In Utility, point out the color borders: **Red** (Overdue), **Amber** (Due Soon), and **Green** (Settled).
2. In Finance, point to the **Net Balance Card** showing Rs. balance, and highlight the color-coded **Budget Alert Banner**.

> **Narrator (Spoken):**
> "Managing bills shouldn't be stressful. The **Utility Tracker** color-codes your bills by urgency: red for overdue, amber for due soon, and green for settled. Plus, it pings your phone three days before a due date.
> 
> Tap over to **Finance**, and you get a complete ledger of your income and expenses. 
> 
> MyLife keeps your budget in check with this dynamic alert banner. If your expenses get too close to your income, the banner changes color to warn you to pump the brakes on spending."

---

### Segment 5: Natural Language Event Planner
**Timing:** `02:05 - 02:40` (35 seconds)  
**Visuals:** Open **Events** (`/future-event`).  
**On-Screen Action:**
1. Show the event card with its real-time countdown timer.
2. Open the **Voice Planner** tool.
3. Paste or type: *"Wedding anniversary next Friday in Colombo at 6:00 PM"*.
4. Point to the auto-extracted fields (Anniversary category, parsed date/time, and location). Click confirm.

> **Narrator (Spoken):**
> "One of my absolute favorite features is the **Voice and Natural Language Event Planner**. 
> 
> Instead of tapping through annoying calendar wheels, you can literally just type or dictate a natural sentence. Let's type: 'Wedding anniversary next Friday in Colombo at 6:00 PM'. 
> 
> The app immediately parses the sentence, sets the category, extracts the date, time, and location, and sets up a live countdown timer. It’s that easy."

---

### Segment 6: Spouse Sync & Wrap-Up
**Timing:** `02:40 - 03:00` (20 seconds)  
**Visuals:** Go back to Home $\rightarrow$ Tap the profile avatar to open Settings $\rightarrow$ Point to the Linked Partner field.  
**On-Screen Action:**
1. Highlight the linked email text field.
2. Return to the Home Dashboard and wave cursor or phone as a sign-off.

> **Narrator (Spoken):**
> "Finally, team up with **Spouse Sync** in Settings. Enter your partner's email, and all shared shopping items, tasks, bills, and events instantly sync across both devices.
> 
> MyLife brings organization, clarity, and peace of mind to your household. Thanks for watching, and start organizing your life today!"

---

## 💡 Quick Tips for the Recording

1. **Pre-populate Data:** Add 2 family members, 2 bills, 1 upcoming event, and a couple of budget transactions before starting so that the screen looks alive.
2. **Smooth Cursor Movement:** Move your pointer or tap intentionally. Wait for the screen transitions to settle before speaking the next line.
3. **Copy-Paste text:** Copy the voice planner text *"Wedding anniversary next Friday in Colombo at 6:00 PM"* ahead of time so you can paste it instantly without typing lag.
